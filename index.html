Researcher · Birmingham, UK

I work on formal models of complex systems under uncertainty — especially where causality, time, and constraints shape what can be inferred and what actions remain feasible. …

Recent work includes: (i) interval-based representations of event time for intermittently connected sensing systems (preserving provenance and queryable uncertainty), (ii) feasibility-set modelling of coordination under latency via a set-valued mapping ECB(C, L, θ) and threshold exclusion results, and (iii) testable hypotheses for coordination via continuous phase gradients.
